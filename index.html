About Us

GAMMA Squash Academy was founded in 2015 by Mohamed Fares, Ahmed Elkholany, and Ahmed Tarek, GAMMA Squash Academy is Port Said’s home for passionate, progressive squash training. Born from a shared vision to build a world-class training hub in our city, we provide expert coaching suitable for every age and level — from juniors learning their first shots to adults improving their fitness and competitive players achieving their goals. Our specialized programs include private coaching sessions, solo training, team training, dedicated footwork sessions and continuous fitness conditioning to develop complete athletes. More than just a court, we are a community where technique, strategy, and love for the game grow together. Welcome to a place where your squash journey is guided by experienced founders and a philosophy that puts your progress first.
